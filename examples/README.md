# Examples — proof, not claims

🌐 English · Deutsch (below)

The internet is full of confident claims about "getting the most out of" an AI assistant. This folder is the opposite: **side-by-side transcripts** showing the same situation with and without the persistent brain.

The scenario is deliberately **neutral and synthetic** — a generic multi-day software task, no personal data — so it demonstrates the mechanism without doxxing anyone. Your real gains show up on *your* work; the point here is the shape of the difference.

| File | What it shows |
|------|---------------|
| [`01-before-without-memory.md`](01-before-without-memory.md) | Day 2 with a stateless chat: the assistant has forgotten everything and re-asks. |
| [`02-after-with-memory.md`](02-after-with-memory.md) | Day 2 with the persistent brain: the assistant resumes mid-stride, no re-explaining. |

Read them as a pair. The difference between them *is* the thesis.

## Deutsch

Das Szenario ist bewusst **neutral und synthetisch** — eine generische mehrtägige Software-Aufgabe, keine persönlichen Daten — damit es den Mechanismus zeigt, ohne jemanden zu deanonymisieren. Deine echten Gewinne zeigen sich an *deiner* Arbeit; hier geht es um die Form des Unterschieds.

Lies die beiden als Paar. Der Unterschied zwischen ihnen *ist* die These.
